Privacy Policy — Cascade

R&S Software · Effective date: 10 June 2026

The short version: Cascade collects no data. Nothing ever leaves your device.

What we collect

Nothing. Cascade has no account or sign-up, no analytics, no advertising SDK, and makes no network calls after the first load. We do not collect, store, or process any personal data — so under the GDPR there is no personal data for us to access, export, correct, or delete.

What stays on your device

Your progress, high scores, daily-challenge streak, and settings are saved in local storage on your own device only. They are never transmitted anywhere and are never shared with us or with any third party.

Third parties

None. No third-party services receive any data from Cascade, and nothing is sold or shared.

How to erase your data

Use the Reset option in the in-game settings, or simply uninstall the app. Either removes all locally stored game data.

Children

Cascade is suitable for a general audience and collects no data from anyone, including children.

Future changes

A future update may introduce advertising; if that happens, this policy will be updated before that version is released.
