## Full description (Google Play, max 4000 chars — also usable as App Store description)

```
Drop a piece. Clear a line. Then watch everything fall.

Cascade is a block puzzle with a twist: when you clear a full row or column, the remaining blocks fall — and if the falling blocks complete new lines, those clear too. Chain reactions, big points, and that "one more run" feeling.

HOW IT PLAYS
• Drag pieces from the tray onto the 8×8 grid
• Fill a full row or column to clear it
• After a clear, blocks fall — chains ("cascades") score extra
• Tap a piece in the tray to rotate it
• Stuck? You get one undo per run — spend it wisely
• Keep clearing to build your combo streak multiplier
• Empty the entire board for a big all-clear bonus

DAILY CHALLENGE
One puzzle a day, the same for every player in the world. Same pieces, same order — only skill decides the score. Come back every day to build your streak.

PLAYS ANYWHERE, ASKS NOTHING
• 100% offline — on a plane, on the metro, off the grid
• No account, no sign-up
• Nothing leaves your device: scores and progress are stored locally
• Tiny download, loads in a blink
• Satisfying haptics and clean, synthesized sound

Short runs, deep decisions. How long can you keep the board alive?

Cascade is made by R&S Software.
```
